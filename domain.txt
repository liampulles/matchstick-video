Things that happen in the store:
* Employees rent out videos to customers
* Employees process returned videos
* Customers take out accounts
* Customers rent videos
* Customers return videos

Resources:
* Inventory
	* Create Videos to inventory
	* Update video information
	* Delete videos from inventory

* Accounts
	* Create Account
	* Read Account information
	* Update Account information
	* Delete Account

* Rentals
	* Create rental (take out video)
	* Read rental (who has taken out video)
	* Delete rental (return video)

* Receipts
	* Create receipt (when renting out video)
	* Read receipt (querying income).
